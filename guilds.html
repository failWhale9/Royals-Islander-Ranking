Inactive boat 2014-Apr --- The oldest surviving Islander guild. The original founder, "boat" was banned for account sharing, so it has since been managed by Chokladkakan, one of the few Jrs in the guild who still played.
Inactive snayl 2014-Dec 2015-Apr Newbology was created after Amherst's leader was banned, giving islanders a new home. The icon was eventually changed from blue to white, a reference to a bug that caused the white rose to be rarer than the higher level blue rose in the 2015 easter event.
Active Crylou 2015-May --- Islanders was created for similar reasons as Newbology, and joined an alliance with Newbology [2] when its roster reached 100 members. When Newbology was disbanded, it caused a glitch for alliance members that reset key bindings on every login, causing players to flee Islanders. This guild made a comeback in 2025, thanks to newly appointed Jrs.
Inactive IslandSlut 2015-Jul --- A guild solely for characters that stay in the tutorial zone. Hasn't seen much activity, mostly because camping isn't very popular.
Inactive saulgoodman 2015-Sep 2016-Feb? Newbology [2] was created as a sister guild alongside Islanders, but it was disbanded for an unknown reason, causing the key binding glitch for members of Islanders. This event would lead to the fall of Islanders and the rise of Piggypatrol.
Active snayl 2016-Mar --- Piggypatrol was created after the Islanders/Newbology key binding bug situation, and had a strong presence until new source, when many islanders left for BestBeginner, probably due to the new source guild glitch. At some point ownership was tranferred to Rally. In 2024, Rally and Princesquidd revived Piggypatrol, hoping it would a place where all active islanders could unify without the need for a guild alliance. It is currently one of the main islander guilds.
Inactive snayl 2016-May --- Snayl remade Newbology a third time, mostly to reserve the name, but some islanders still joined at the time.
Inactive ? 2016-Oct --- This guild (spelled "Isianders") existed at the same time as the original Islanders guild. Not much is recorded about it, but it appears to have been created in early October 2016 alongside the original Islanders guild, probably as overflow.
Inactive Sawngo 2016-Nov 2016-Dec Originally envisioned to be a guild to hold the top 100 highest level islanders. It enjoyed a brief surge of popularity until it imploded due to some drama a month later, and was eventually dissolved.
Inactive Delfino 2016-Dec --- BongLords was created for one of Delfino's off-island characters, but gradually gained a small islander following. In December of 2016, BongLords saw a rise in membership when Escargot dissolved. It remained a core Islander guild until July 2017, when most members left due to the new source guild bug.
Inactive DumbIsland 2017-Jul --- Originally made by DumbIsland's off-island perma-beginner "LowDmg" in late 2016. While it began as a perma-beginner guild, it was adopted by islanders in mid-2017 as they fled guilds affected by the new source guild bug. By December of 2017, it seems to have dropped off.
Active CrimsonClaw (now "Islander") 2017-Nov --- After snayl and other islanders left the server due to drama, two major island guilds (Piggypatrol and Newbology) were leaderless. To fix this, Crimsonclaw started Shroomies, and it has had a consistent presence on the island ever since.
Inactive deadppl 2018-May --- In August of 2018, the guild leader was banned, although Islander continued to be a major island guild for several years since there were active Jrs keepng it alive.
Active Lowly 2020-Jul --- In the summer of 2020, Lowly decided to make Island as a guild that would have consistent online leadership at a time where other guilds were not as active. It remains one of the main active guilds to this day.
Inactive NamoAmituofo 2022-Jun --- Namo created this guild because the previous guild he was in (Islander) was full, and many islanders have joined in the year the followed. Interest in the guild waned when namo stopped playing, and many joined Piggypatrol, Shroomies, or Island.
Inactive Adaryn 2023-Jun 2024-Sep Originally the guild "JellyBabies" was created as a sort of bridge to help new players and had a presence both on Maple Island and off-island. Eventually, it was decided the guild should be split, making JellyBabies an off-island guild and creating "Stay" as an Islander guild. The guild was eventually abanoned, due to a combination of Adaryn stepping back from Royals and the push for Piggypatrol to be the sole islander guild.
